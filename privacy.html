Who We Are

The CICS CSU Piat QR Code Generator is a personal educational project developed by Jeylo Baoit. This website helps students quickly generate QR codes for identification purposes.

1. What Data We Collect

We collect NO personal information. All QR code generation happens locally on your device in your browser.

  • ✅ Your ID and Name are processed by your browser only
  • ✅ QR codes are generated on your device
  • ✅ Nothing is sent to our servers
  • ✅ No cookies are used for tracking

2. Browser Storage (Local & Safe)

This website uses localStorage to save non-personal information on your device only:

Important: This data is stored only on your device and is never shared with us or any third parties.
