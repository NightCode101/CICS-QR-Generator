How to Use NFC

📱 Native Mobile NFC (Android Only)

Requires Google Chrome and an NFC-enabled Android device.

  • To Write: Enter the Student ID and Name, click "Write to Tag", and hold the blank card to the back of your phone.
  • To Read: Click "Scan Native NFC" and hold the card to the back of your phone.

🔌 External USB Reader (PCs & iPhones)

For devices without native Web NFC support.

  • Seamless Scanning: Ensure your USB RFID reader is plugged in.
  • You do not need to click anything on the screen.
  • Simply tap the ID card on the USB reader at any time, and the system will automatically catch the data in the background.
