Executive Summary

CICS Attendance Control ("App") is a real-time attendance tracking application for students and educators at CSU Piat CICS (College of Information and Computing Sciences). The app uses camera-based QR scanning and NFC/RFID tags for efficient attendance verification. This policy details exactly what data we collect, why, how long we keep it, and your privacy rights. We prioritize user privacy and minimize data collection.

1. Who We Are

CICS Attendance Control is developed by NightCode Labs - Jeylo Baoit to provide secure, transparent attendance tracking for CSU Piat CICS students and staff.

  • Organization: NightCode Labs
  • Developer: NightCode Labs - Jeylo Baoit
  • Contact: nightcodelabs101@gmail.com

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Attendance Check-In Data (Core Feature)

  • Student ID: Unique identifier for each student
  • Timestamp: Date and time of check-in
  • Class/Course ID: Which course or session attended
  • Location: Building or room where attendance was recorded
  • Purpose: Generate official attendance records for grading
  • Storage: Encrypted Firebase Firestore database
  • Retention: Stored indefinitely (per academic records requirements) or until student deletion

2.2 Camera Access (QR Code Scanning)

  • Permission: App requests camera permission when student taps "Scan QR"
  • What's Collected: Real-time video stream from device camera
  • Purpose: Decode QR codes to extract student ID for attendance verification
  • Processing: Camera feed analyzed locally in app using JavaScript QR decoder
  • Storage: ❌ NEVER stored or recorded — live stream only
  • Server Transmission: ❌ NO — camera feed stays on your device
  • Duration: Permission active only during scan; user can revoke anytime in device settings
  • How to Revoke:
    • iOS: Settings > Privacy > Camera > CICS Attendance Control > Deny
    • Android: Settings > Apps > CICS Attendance Control > Permissions > Camera > Deny

2.3 NFC/RFID Tag Access (Attendance Check-In)

  • Permission: App requests NFC access when "NFC Check-In" is enabled
  • What's Collected: RFID/NFC tag data (typically: Student ID + encrypted metadata)
  • Purpose: Read attendance data from NFC badges/tags for quick check-in without scanning
  • Processing: NFC data read locally; synced to Firebase with encryption
  • Storage: Encrypted in Firebase Firestore (see section 2.4.2)
  • Retention: Per academic records policy (indefinite unless student deletion requested)
  • Security: NFC reader protected by faculty admin password
  • How to Revoke:
    • iOS: Settings > Privacy > NFC > CICS Attendance Control > Deny
    • Android: Settings > Apps > CICS Attendance Control > Permissions > NFC > Deny

2.4 Firebase Services

2.4.1 Firebase Authentication (Auth)

  • Data Collected: Email (university email), password hash, phone number (optional)
  • Purpose: Secure user account creation, session management, access control
  • Storage: Encrypted on Firebase servers (Google Cloud)
  • Processing: Auth tokens stored in app's secure storage for session persistence
  • Retention: Active while account exists; deleted 30 days after account deletion request
  • Third-party Sharing: ❌ NOT shared with third parties
  • Encryption: bcrypt hashing for passwords; TLS 1.2+ in transit

2.4.2 Firebase Firestore (Primary Database)

  • Data Stored:
    • Student profiles (ID, name, major, email)
    • Attendance records (timestamp, course, status)
    • NFC/RFID tag mappings to student IDs
    • Course information and class schedules
    • Teacher profiles and course assignments
    • Notifications and messages
  • Purpose: Central persistent storage for all app data across devices
  • Storage Location: Google Cloud data centers (multi-region for redundancy)
  • Encryption: AES-256 encryption at rest; TLS in transit
  • Retention: Stored indefinitely per academic records policy; deletable by students/admins
  • Deletion Timeline: Full deletion 7 days after user deletion request (after backup purge)
  • Backup & Recovery: Automated daily backups retained 7 days; then permanently deleted

2.4.3 Firebase Cloud Functions

  • Purpose: Server-side processing for:
    • Bulk attendance imports from CSV files
    • Automated attendance calculations and reports
    • Email notifications to students/teachers
    • Late attendance flags and exceptions
  • Data Accessed: Limited to necessary parameters (student ID, timestamp, course ID)
  • Logs Retention: Retained 30 days for debugging; automatically purged
  • No Long-term Storage: ❌ Functions do NOT permanently store input data
  • Timeout: All functions timeout after 5 minutes to prevent runaway processing

2.4.4 Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM - Push Notifications)

  • Purpose: Send push notifications for:
    • Late attendance alerts ("You're 10 minutes late")
    • Class cancellations or schedule changes
    • Teacher messages and announcements
    • Marked as absent notifications
  • Data Collected: Device FCM token (anonymous device identifier, NOT tied to personal info)
  • Storage: FCM tokens stored in Firestore mapped to student accounts
  • Opt-out: Disable push notifications in app Settings > Notifications OR in OS notification settings
  • Retention: Token deleted when user disables notifications or deletes account

2.4.5 Firebase Analytics

  • Data Collected:
    • App screen views (which features used, when)
    • Feature usage (QR scans, NFC check-ins, report generation)
    • Device type, OS version, app version
    • Language, country, device model
    • Session duration, crash events
    • ❌ NO personally identifiable information (name, email, Student ID)
  • Purpose: Understand feature adoption, stability, and performance; identify bugs
  • Retention: 14 months default (configurable in app settings)
  • Opt-out: App Settings > Privacy > Disable Analytics

2.4.6 Firebase In-App Messaging

  • When Enabled: App may display contextual messages to users (e.g., "Mark your attendance now")
  • Data Collected: Message impression counts, user interactions with messages
  • Purpose: Guide users to features, announce schedule changes, collect feedback
  • Storage: Message impression data retained 30 days
  • Opt-out: Settings > In-App Messaging > Disable

2.5 Google Analytics

  • Data Collected: Page views, features used, device info, general location (country/city), session duration
  • ❌ NOT Collected: Personal identifiable information (names, emails, Student IDs)
  • Cookies Used: Yes, for session and attribution tracking
  • Purpose: Monitor app health, identify popular features, diagnose performance issues
  • Retention: 14 months default
  • Opt-out: Use browser privacy mode, install privacy extension, disable JavaScript
  • More Info: Google Analytics Privacy Policy

2.6 Google AdMob (In-App Advertisements)

  • Purpose: Display targeted advertisements within the app to support development
  • Data Collected:
    • Advertising ID (anonymous device identifier)
    • Device type, OS version, country, language
    • Ad impression data (how many ads shown, user interactions)
    • Estimated age range and inferred interests (NOT confirmed)
    • ❌ NO name, email, Student ID, or other personal info
  • Cookies & Tracking: Google may set cookies for ad personalization
  • Retention: Per Google AdMob policy (typically 24 months)
  • Opt-out of Personalized Ads:
    • iOS: Settings > Privacy > Apple Advertising > Personalized Ads > Toggle OFF
    • Android: Settings > Google > Manage your Google Account > Data & Privacy > Ad Settings > Personalization > OFF
    • Web: Visit Google Privacy Controls
  • Ad Choices: Ad privacy icon in top-left corner of ads for additional opt-outs
  • More Info: Google AdMob Privacy Policy

2.7 Google Funding Choices (UMP - Consent Management)

  • Purpose: Display privacy consent messages for GDPR (EU), CCPA (California), and other regional regulations
  • When Used: If accessing from EU, California, UK, Canada, or other regulated regions
  • Data Collected: User consent choices (Accept personalized ads / Reject personalized ads / etc.)
  • Storage: Consent preference stored in cookies for 30 days
  • Reference: Google's UMP Privacy & Consent Policy

2.8 Device & Location Data

  • GPS Location: ❌ NOT collected or requested
  • Approximate Location: Inferred from IP address for analytics (country/city level only)
  • Building/Room Location: ✅ Optionally recorded if teacher manually tags attendance location
  • IP Address: Collected in server logs; purged after 30 days
  • Device Model & OS: Collected for analytics; never personally identifiable

2.9 Server Logs & Technical Data

  • Data Collected: IP address, request URL, timestamp, browser user-agent, HTTP status codes
  • Purpose: Diagnose server errors, detect abuse, monitor uptime
  • Retention: 30 days (automatically purged)
  • Sharing: ❌ NOT shared with third parties

2.10 Data NOT Collected

  • ❌ Biometric data (facial recognition, fingerprints)
  • ❌ Password (except during signup, stored encrypted)
  • ❌ Health or medical information
  • ❌ Payment methods or financial data
  • ❌ Precise GPS coordinates
